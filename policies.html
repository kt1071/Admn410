Financing

1 THE SHOPPING CART

To view your shopping cart, click the 'Cart' button at the top right portion of your screen. To add items to your cart, simply select an item you want and click 'add to cart' on the product page. Once in your cart, you can modify the quantity of the items you have added, remove items, enter coupons and discounts, and select your shipping type.

2 COMPLETING YOUR ORDER & CHECKOUT

Once you have reviewed the items in your cart, click the 'Proceed to Checkout' to begin the quick and simple process of checking out and placing your order. You will be asked for some contact and billing information. If you are placing a prescription order, you may be asked to enter some information about your pet and veterinarian so we can ensure the accuracy of your prescription order. Next review your order, make sure everything is correct, and click 'submit'. Print the receipt for your records. We will also e-mail you a copy of your order to keep for your records.

3 ACCEPTED PAYMENT METHODS

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, and now PayPal. We also accept personal checks and money orders via mail. Please make all checks and money orders payable to 'Durham House of Pest' and send them to 18 Library Way, Durham, NH 03824.

Delivery

Durham House of Pets ships pet food to the 48 continental states only.

Any orders placed with Durham House of Pets for pet food, will be shipped within the next business day.

Pet food will ship Monday through Friday.

If an order is placed on Friday, it will not be shipped until the following business day (Monday) as we do not ship on Saturday and Sunday.

Shipping charges on pet food or orders over 35 lbs are based on accumulated weights.

Orders placed for pet food weighing over 35 lbs will only be shipped via UPS Ground, which would be expected to be delivered within 5 to 7 business days once shipped.

You must have a street address for delivery. Pet food cannot be shipped to an APO/APE or PO Box, since UPS is used for delivery.

If you order pet food along with other items, please be aware that you will receive your pet food order in a separate shipment. You will not incur additional shipping charges.

Vaccines are shipped Next-Day, but orders placed on Friday, Saturday, or Sunday for vaccines will not ship until Monday.
